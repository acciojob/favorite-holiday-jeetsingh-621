Paris, France

Paris is known for its art, fashion, and culture. Famous landmarks, museums, and cafes make it a top travel spot.

  1. Eiffel Tower: Iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame: A Gothic masterpiece.
  4. Champs-Élysées: Famous avenue for shopping and cafes.
  5. Sacré-Cœur: Stunning views from Montmartre.
  • Croissants: Flaky and buttery.
  • Baguette: Fresh and crusty.
  • Macarons: Colorful French sweets.
  • Escargots: Snails in garlic butter.
  • Ratatouille: Classic vegetable dish.
